# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or political discussions
- Publishing private information
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact:
- Email: conduct@agroshield.io
- Discord: Contact moderators directly

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:
- Warning from community moderators
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal from contribution privileges

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all community spaces including:
- GitHub repositories
- Discord server
- Official social media channels
- Community events and meetups
