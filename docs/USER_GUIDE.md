*Last updated: April 23, 2026*

## Getting Started

Welcome to AgroShield! This guide will help you navigate our decentralized crop insurance platform.

## Account Setup

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

- **MetaMask**: Install MetaMask browser extension
- **MiniPay**: Use MiniPay mobile wallet (Celo users)
- **WalletConnect**: Connect mobile wallets to desktop

### 2. Switch to Celo Network

- Network Name: Celo Mainnet
- RPC URL: https://forno.celo.org
- Chain ID: 42220
- Currency Symbol: CELO

### 3. Get Test Funds

For testing, visit the [Celo Faucet](https://celo.org/developers/faucet) to get test CELO and cUSD.

## Creating Insurance Policies

### Step 1: Navigate to Policies
1. Click "Policies" in the sidebar
2. Click "Create New Policy"

### Step 2: Fill Policy Details
- **Crop Type**: Select your crop (wheat, corn, soybeans, etc.)
- **Location**: Choose your farming location
- **Coverage Amount**: Enter desired coverage in USD
- **Duration**: Select policy duration (3, 6, 9, or 12 months)
- **Rainfall Threshold**: Set minimum rainfall requirement

### Step 3: Review and Confirm
- Review calculated premium
- Check risk assessment
- Confirm transaction details
- Approve wallet transaction

### Step 4: Policy Activation
- Pay premium to activate policy
- Receive confirmation
- Policy is now active

## Managing Claims

### Submitting a Claim

1. **Navigate to Claims**: Click "Claims" in sidebar
2. **File New Claim**: Click "Submit Claim"
3. **Select Policy**: Choose the policy to claim against
4. **Claim Details**:
   - Claim type (drought, flood, pest damage, etc.)
   - Incident date and location
   - Description of the incident
   - Estimated loss amount
5. **Upload Evidence**: Add supporting documents
6. **Review Payout**: See estimated payout amount
7. **Submit**: Approve transaction to submit claim

### Tracking Claim Status

Claims go through these stages:
- **Submitted**: Claim received and under review
- **Processing**: Weather data being verified
- **Approved**: Claim approved for payout
- **Paid**: Payout processed and sent
- **Rejected**: Claim rejected with reason

## Providing Liquidity

### Adding Liquidity

1. **Navigate to Liquidity**: Click "Liquidity" in sidebar
2. **Provide Liquidity**: Click "Add Liquidity"
3. **Choose Token**: Select CELO or cUSD
4. **Enter Amount**: Specify amount to provide
5. **Select Duration**: Choose lock duration for bonus APY
6. **Review Rewards**: See estimated rewards
7. **Confirm**: Approve transaction

### Withdrawing Liquidity

1. **Go to Liquidity**: Navigate to liquidity section
2. **View Positions**: See your current positions
3. **Withdraw**: Click "Withdraw" next to position
4. **Enter Amount**: Specify amount to withdraw
5. **Confirm**: Approve transaction

### Liquidity Rewards

- **Base APY**: Standard yield for providing liquidity
- **Duration Bonus**: Additional yield for longer lock periods
- **Auto-Compound**: Automatically reinvest rewards for higher returns

## Understanding Risk Factors

### Crop Risk Levels
- **Low Risk**: Wheat, Rice (0.25x multiplier)
- **Medium Risk**: Corn, Soybeans (0.35-0.40x multiplier)
- **High Risk**: Cotton, Coffee (0.45-0.50x multiplier)

### Location Risk
- **Low Risk**: Midwest USA (1.0x multiplier)
- **Medium Risk**: California, Texas (1.1-1.2x multiplier)
- **High Risk**: International locations (1.3-1.5x multiplier)

### Weather Conditions
- **Low Rainfall**: Higher premiums, higher payout probability
- **Normal Rainfall**: Standard premiums
- **High Rainfall**: Lower premiums, lower payout probability

### Transaction Security

### Smart Contract Security
- Interact only with verified contracts

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Transaction Failed**
- Check gas price and limit
- Verify wallet balance
- Ensure correct network (Celo Mainnet)
- Try refreshing the page

**Policy Not Showing**
- Wait for transaction confirmation
- Refresh the dashboard
- Check transaction on CeloScan
- Contact support if issues persist

**Claim Processing Delay**
- Weather data verification takes time
- Check claim status regularly
- Provide additional evidence if requested
- Allow 24-48 hours for processing
